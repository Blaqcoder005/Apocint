About Apostolic Chapel Int'l

(Habitation of Grace)

Who We Are

Apostolic Chapel International (Habitation of Grace) was founded on Thursday, November 8, 2000 by Apostle Joshua Oluseye Olayiwola in Ibadan, Nigeria. The vision began in 1998/99 at Sarumi Mountain Airport as a weekly program, and grew into full services in 2001.

The Church journeyed from Iyana Church to Isebo in 2003, introduced Bible Study in 2004, and endured a fire outbreak in 2005 — giving birth to the annual Passover Anniversary revival. The current church site was opened in 2008, and the building completed by 2014.

Major programs include the Ministers’ Vigil (2003), “My Redeemer Liveth” deliverance series (2016), and “God at Sinai” empowerment program (2001). Apostle Joshua has authored books including Noah’s Ark and Be at Peace, and was awarded a Doctorate in Theology in 2014.

With global reach across Africa, Europe, and the U.S., the ministry remains committed to soul-winning, deliverance, and raising end-time ministers under a prophetic apostolic mantle.

Our Mission

To raise empowered, Spirit-filled believers through Apostolic teaching, prophetic impartation, and transformational programs — bringing salvation, healing, and deliverance to individuals, families, and nations.

Our Vision

To be a global Apostolic and prophetic movement — restoring lives, reviving destinies, and equipping ministers for the end-time harvest through the Word of God, Holy Spirit-led programs, and supernatural power.

What We Believe

  • In One True God—Father, Son, and Holy Spirit.
  • In Jesus Christ, the Savior who died and rose again for us.
  • In the Holy Spirit’s power to sanctify and empower believers.
  • In the Bible as the inspired and final authority of truth.
  • In prophetic and Apostolic calling for edification of the Church.
  • In healing, deliverance, and revival as God’s agenda for restoration.
  • In fulfilling the Great Commission to preach the Gospel worldwide.

About the Founder

Apostle Joshua Oluseye Olayiwola is a visionary leader with a prophetic mandate to equip saints and build spiritual foundations. He has authored impactful books and has ministered across nations including Kenya, the U.S., Uganda, and the U.K.
